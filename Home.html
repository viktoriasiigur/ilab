About me

My name is Viktoria. I was born ten days before year 2000 so I am 21 years old right now. I am currently studying IT systems development in Tallinn University of Technology and during the summer I worked as a software developer in a small company called Devtailor. 

I have several hobbies, but one of my biggest addictions is music. I have played the guitar for about 12 years, I am also bassist in three orchestras. Sometimes, when needed, I play the drums. I also have an athletic hobby - disc-golf. If somebody does not know - the whole point of the game is to throw a disc into a specific basket. I have been doing this for about 4 years. 

More fun facts about me:
* I am from the smallest town in Estonia called Mõisaküla, which population is a little     bit over 700; 
* My height is 184 cm.
* Few years ago I participated in Estonian scientific TV show, which I almost won.

Why did I want to participate in iLab? There were several reasons. First of all I wanted to raise my self-confidence. Working in teams and being forced to talk with others did it perfectly. Also I wanted to improve my English and get basic skills for speaking in German. As well it is great to get friends from different fields of life and that was also one of my goals for iLab semester.
